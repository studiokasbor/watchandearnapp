Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Watch & Earn account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to delete your account

Option 1 — In the app:

  1. Open Watch & Earn
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice

Option 2 — By email:

Send an email to studiokasbor@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

What data is deleted

  • ✅ Your account and login credentials
  • ✅ Your profile information (username, email)
  • ✅ Your coin balance and transaction history
  • ✅ Your referral data

What data is retained

  • ⚠️ Completed payout records are retained for 90 days for legal and fraud prevention purposes
  • ⚠️ Anonymous usage statistics (not linked to your account)

Processing time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.

Contact

Questions? Email us at studiokasbor@gmail.com
